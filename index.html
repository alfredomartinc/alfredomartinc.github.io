I am a Ph.D. in Economics candidate at Georgia State University. My research interest is in the intersection between applied econometrics, labor economics, and Human Rights.
My dissertation studies the effect of technical education on unemployment duration and the role of economic constraints in the self-selection into technical colleges. With my advisor, Dr. Daniel Kreisman, I have also been working on Career and Technical Education in partnership with the Michigan Department of Education and the University of Michigan.
